Interesting Points

You may wonder, "Why is Brazil mentioned in a story set in the 5th/6th centuries?" It turns out that "Brazil" comes from the phrase "Hy Brazil," which is a mythical island west of Ireland.

How about America? According to Wikipedia, T. H. White's writing style made use of anachronisms, of which this is one.

There he hung or hoved, his vast ironic mouth permanently drawn downward in a kind of melancholy, his lean clean-shaven chops giving him an American expression, like that of Uncle Sam.
