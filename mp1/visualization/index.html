About this Chart

I ran a linear regression on the relative frequencies by chapter of each word in The Once and Future King, with the chapter number as the independent variable and the relative frequency within the chapter as the dependent variable. The upper graph shows the words with regressions with the most extreme slopes, representing the greatest increase or decrease in the frequency of occurrence of the word over the course of the novel. Clicking on a particular word displays on the lower graph a breakdown of its relative frequency by chapter.
